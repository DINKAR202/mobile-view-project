Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering

SJCE - An Overview

Sri Jayachamarajendra College Of Engineering, conceived in 1963, is the dream child of Jagadguru Dr. Sri Shivarathri Rajendra Mahaswamigalavaru, the 23rd pontiff of Sri Suttur Mutt. As one of the leading institutes in India, SJCE has been recognized under the Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme(TEQIP). The institution also has the reputation of academic excellence in professionally oriented programs, and equal proficiency in extra-curricular activities.

Approval and Accreditation

SJCE is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
All its departments are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation.
SJCE is affiliated to JSS Science and Technology University.
Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering is the best engineering college in Mysore as per the NIRF ranking.

Reasons to Study at SJCE?

SJCE Selected as Lead Institute for Assistance by World Bank under TEQIP Scheme of Government available.

The college undertakes several development activities. Facilities including excellent Computer Centre, Library, WorkShops etc are provided for research programs leading to M.Sc (Engg) and Ph.D in Electrical Sciences, Mathematics, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Polymer Science and Technology are available.

College has been identified as one of the Centres of the Network scheme envisaged by the Government of Indian.

The college maintains a host of Computer Systems throughout the campus and operates 24X7 basis. Internet facility is also provided.

SJCE maintains a good Student Faculty Management relationship.

Thus the college has carved a niche for itself as a premier centre for Technical Education.

Eligibility Criteria

Under-Graduate

B.E

Passed the 2nd PUC/ 12th / Equivalent Exam with English as one of the languages with a minimum of 45% of marks in aggregate in Physics and Mathematics along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology / Biology / Electronics/ Computers. (40% for Karnataka reserved category candidates).

Taking any one entrance exam is Mandatory. CET/ COMED-K / JEE / AIEEE.

Post-Graduate

MTech (Technology/CSE/VLSI Design & Embedded Systems)

Relevant BE/BTech degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for Karnataka SC/ST category) from a recognized university.

Entrance Exam (anyone): PGCET/GATE in core specialisation.

Campus Life

Hostel: The housing (hostel) facilities are made as ‘homely’ as possible. With choices of single / shared furnished occupancies with or without kitchenettes. The life at the hostel is an extension of the academic pursuit of the student.

Library: The Learning Resource Centre (LRC) – the Central Library, at the heart of the Campus, provides an ideal environment for intellectual inquiry with its multiple Study Spaces, Research Assistance, and Reading, Learning and Instructional Resources.

Sport's : SJCE always encourages sports in a big way. In fact, the ideals of SJCE founders that sports should be a part of engineering education has been maintained even today.

Culture : SJCE believes in creating a generation of students who are not only academically brilliant but also talented in subjects outside of the classroom.

Wifi & Internet: The whole campus is connected over the internet and students, faculty and college guests can connect to the internet with high bandwidth wifi from any corner of the Placement.
